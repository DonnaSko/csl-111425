# How to Find Your Actual App URL

## Step 1: Get Your App URL from DigitalOcean

1. Go to: https://cloud.digitalocean.com/apps
2. Click on your app
3. Look at the top of the page - you'll see your app's URL
4. It will look something like:
   - `https://csl-bjg7z.ondigitalocean.app` (your actual URL)
   - Or `https://your-app-name-xxxxx.ondigitalocean.app`

## Step 2: Test With Your Actual URL

Once you have your actual URL, test these:

- `https://YOUR-ACTUAL-URL.ondigitalocean.app/login`
- `https://YOUR-ACTUAL-URL.ondigitalocean.app/register`

Replace `YOUR-ACTUAL-URL` with what you see in DigitalOcean.

## Quick Way to Find It

In DigitalOcean dashboard:
- Click on your app
- Look for a button that says "Live App" or "Visit App" 
- Or look at the top where it shows the app name and URL
- The URL is usually displayed prominently

## Example

If your app URL is `https://csl-bjg7z.ondigitalocean.app`, then test:
- `https://csl-bjg7z.ondigitalocean.app/login`
- `https://csl-bjg7z.ondigitalocean.app/register`
