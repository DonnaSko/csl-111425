## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 To Provide the Service
- Authenticate your account
- Store and manage your leads and data
- Send emails on your behalf
- Generate reports and analytics
- Provide customer support
- Process payments
- Maintain platform security

### 3.2 Community Benchmarking & Gamification
**This is a core feature of our Service.**

#### What We Do:
- **Collect performance metrics from your account** (as listed in Section 2.2)
- **Aggregate your metrics with ALL other users' metrics anonymously**
- **Calculate percentile rankings** (e.g., "You're in the top 25%")
- **Display community averages** to all users
- **Show your personal metrics compared to community benchmarks**

#### Why We Do This:
- To motivate users to improve lead management
- To provide industry insights and performance context
- To gamify the experience and increase engagement
- To help users understand best practices

#### Anonymization Process:
- **ALL personally identifiable information is removed** before aggregation
- **NO company names, user names, or identities are revealed** to other users
- Only aggregate statistics are calculated (averages, percentiles)
- Individual user data is NEVER shared with other users
- Reverse-engineering individual data from aggregates is mathematically infeasible

#### What Other Users See:
- Community average metrics (e.g., "Average quality score: 6.1")
- Their own percentile ranking (e.g., "You're in the 72nd percentile")
- Total number of companies in the benchmark (e.g., "42 CSL users")
- **Other users CANNOT see:**
  - Your company name
  - Your specific metrics
  - Your leads or data
  - Any identifying information

### 3.3 To Improve the Service
- Analyze usage patterns to improve features
- Identify and fix bugs
- Develop new features based on user behavior
- Optimize performance and user experience
- Conduct A/B testing (anonymously)

### 3.4 Communications
- Send transactional emails (e.g., password resets, payment confirmations)
- Send service updates and important notices
- Respond to your support requests
- Send optional marketing emails (you can opt out)

### 3.5 Legal Compliance
- Comply with legal obligations
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Protect our rights and property
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Respond to law enforcement requests

## 7. Your Privacy Rights

### 7.1 GDPR Rights (European Users)
If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to:
- **Access** - Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification** - Correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure** - Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restriction** - Limit how we process your data
- **Portability** - Export your data in a machine-readable format
- **Object** - Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Withdraw Consent** - Withdraw consent at any time (by canceling your account)

### 7.2 CCPA Rights (California Users)
If you are in California, you have the right to:
- **Know** - Request disclosure of data collected, used, and shared
- **Delete** - Request deletion of your personal data
- **Opt-Out** - Opt out of data sales (we don't sell data, so this doesn't apply)
- **Non-Discrimination** - We will not discriminate for exercising your rights

### 7.3 How to Exercise Your Rights
Contact us at:
- Email: privacy@captureshowleads.com
- Include your account email and specific request
- We will respond within **30 days**
- We may verify your identity before fulfilling requests

### 7.4 Opting Out of Community Benchmarking
**Community benchmarking is a core feature of the Service.**
- If you do not consent to anonymous data aggregation, you must cancel your account
- There is no way to use the Service without participating in benchmarking
- Your only opt-out is to not use the Service
