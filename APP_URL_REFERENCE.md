# Your App URL - Quick Reference

## üöÄ How to Find Your App URL

### Quick Steps:
1. Go to: **https://cloud.digitalocean.com/apps**
2. Click on your app (should be named `csl-app` or similar)
3. Look at the top of the page for your app's URL
4. It will look like: `https://csl-xxxxx.ondigitalocean.app`

### Alternative Method:
1. In DigitalOcean dashboard, click on your app
2. Look for a **"Live App"** or **"Visit App"** button
3. Click it - that's your app URL!

---

## üìù Your App URL Format

Your app URL will be in this format:
```
https://[app-name]-[random-id].ondigitalocean.app
```

**Example:**
- `https://csl-bjg7z.ondigitalocean.app`
- `https://csl-app-abc123.ondigitalocean.app`

## üîç Can't Find It?

If you can't find your app URL:
1. Make sure you're logged into DigitalOcean
2. Check that your app is deployed (not in "building" state)
3. Look in the app's **Settings** tab for the URL
4. Check the **Components** tab - the frontend component should show the URL

---

**Note:** Your app URL is unique to your DigitalOcean account and app. 
The exact URL will be shown in your DigitalOcean dashboard.
