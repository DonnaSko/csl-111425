# How to Access DigitalOcean Console - Step by Step

## Method 1: Through Components Tab

1. **Go to DigitalOcean Dashboard**
   - URL: https://cloud.digitalocean.com
   - Login if needed

2. **Click "Apps" in left sidebar**
   - You'll see a list of your apps

3. **Click on your app name**
   - Should be something like "csl-111425" or "csl-app"

4. **Click "Components" tab** (or "Runtime" tab)
   - At the top of the page

5. **Click on "backend" component**
   - You'll see a list of components (backend, frontend, database)
   - Click on the one named "backend"

6. **Look for "Console" tab or button**
   - Should be at the top, next to "Settings", "Logs", etc.
   - OR look for a button that says "Run Console" or "Open Console"

7. **Click "Console"**
   - A terminal window will open

8. **Paste your command**
   - You'll see a prompt like `$` or `#`
   - Paste the command and press Enter

## Method 2: Through Settings Tab

1. **Go to your app** (same as above)

2. **Click "Settings" tab**

3. **Look for "Console" or "Run Console"**
   - Might be under "Component Settings" or "Runtime"

4. **Click it to open terminal**

## Method 3: Through Activity Tab

1. **Go to your app**

2. **Click "Activity" tab**

3. **Find a recent deployment**

4. **Click "View details"**

5. **Look for "Console" option**

## What the Console Looks Like

- Black or dark background
- White text
- A prompt like: `$` or `#` or `root@...`
- You can type commands here

## If You Can't Find Console

**Alternative:** Use the "Runtime Logs" tab to see what's happening, but you can't run commands there.
